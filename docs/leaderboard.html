TipWhat Feature Importance Tells Us

The XGBoost feature importance confirms the Alpha Architect paper’s key finding:

  1. Chronological features dominate — c13 (day 13) alone accounts for ~48% of gain
  2. Mid-month timing matters most — days 10-17 are the most predictive window
  3. Rank extremes add signal — r5 (5th lowest) and r21 (highest) contribute
  4. Recent days are NOT the most important — c20, c21 have low importance, contradicting simple momentum

This suggests the signal is driven by institutional rebalancing patterns (mid-month flows) rather than simple recency or momentum.
